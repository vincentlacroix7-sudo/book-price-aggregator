I find that when you are looking for the price of a book, there are no websites that really do it well. So my goal is to create a book price aggregator and make it very modern. It will have prices for new and used book. It will have to run a software that automatically compare and check price for books. My inspiration for this is gg.deals. I would monetize the entire project with affiliate links and with ads on the website and in the future maybe partnerships with book publishers.
